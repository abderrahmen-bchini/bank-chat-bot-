# Wifak Bank - Employee Handbook

**Document Reference:** HR-HBK-001  
**Effective Year:** 2026  
**Version:** 5.0  
**Owner:** Human Resources Department  

---

## 1. Introduction & Core Values
Welcome to Wifak Bank. Since our establishment, our mission has been to provide secure, innovative, and reliable financial solutions to our retail and commercial clients. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of integrity, customer focus, and operational excellence. 

Our core values drive everything we do:
1. **Integrity First:** We do what is right, transparent, and compliant under all regulatory frameworks.
2. **Security & Trust:** We protect our clients' data and assets with the utmost diligence.
3. **Innovation:** We continuously improve our digital banking capabilities and internal tools.
4. **Inclusive Collaboration:** We foster a diverse work environment where everyone is valued.

---

## 2. Working Hours and Attendance
* **Standard Working Hours:** The official working hours for Wifak Bank are **9:00 AM to 5:00 PM**, Monday through Friday. This includes a 1-hour unpaid lunch break.
* **Core Hours:** All employees are expected to be available during the core collaboration hours of **10:00 AM to 3:00 PM**.
* **Flexible Working:** Employees may arrange alternative start/end times (e.g., 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM) with their direct manager's written approval, provided they meet their 35-hour weekly requirement.

---

## 3. Remote and Hybrid Work Policy
Wifak Bank operates under a **hybrid work model** designed to balance flexibility with the benefits of in-person collaboration.
* **Office Requirement:** Employees are expected to work from their designated local office at least **3 days per week**.
* **Remote Days:** Employees may work remotely up to **2 days per week** (typically Mondays and Fridays, unless team coverage dictates otherwise).
* **Equipment Allowance:** Wifak Bank provides a one-time home office setup allowance of up to **$500** for permanent employees to purchase ergonomic chairs, monitors, or keyboards. Receipts must be submitted to the IT/Facilities department for reimbursement.

---

## 4. Code of Conduct and Ethics
* **Professional Attire:** Employees should maintain a "business casual" dress code when in the office or on video calls with clients.
* **Conflict of Interest:** Employees must declare any personal financial interests, external employment, or business relationships that may conflict with the bank's interests. Annual declaration via the compliance portal is mandatory.
* **Gift Policy:** Employees must not accept gifts, entertainment, or hospitality from clients or suppliers exceeding **$50** in value. Any gifts received must be registered with the Compliance department within 5 business days.
* **Confidentiality:** Bank and customer records are strictly confidential. Sharing non-public financial or customer data externally is a serious offense and grounds for immediate termination.

---

## 5. Performance Management
* **Cycle:** Performance evaluations at Wifak Bank run on a calendar year cycle.
* **Mid-Year Check-In:** Conducted in June to review goals, receive feedback, and adjust priorities.
* **Annual Review:** Conducted in December, resulting in a performance rating of 1 to 5:
  * *5 - Outstanding Performance*
  * *4 - Exceeds Expectations*
  * *3 - Meets Expectations*
  * *2 - Needs Improvement*
  * *1 - Unsatisfactory*
* **Development Plans:** Employees receiving a rating of 2 or below will be placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) lasting 60 to 90 days.

---

## 6. Compensation & Benefits
* **Pay Cycle:** Salaries are paid monthly on the **25th of each month** via direct deposit. If the 25th falls on a weekend or holiday, payment is made on the preceding business day.
* **Retirement Plan:** Wifak Bank matches employee contributions to the retirement fund dollar-for-dollar up to **5% of base salary**, vesting immediately upon completing 1 year of service.
* **Health & Wellness:** Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance is provided. HR also coordinates an annual wellness stipend of $300 for gym memberships or wellness activities.
