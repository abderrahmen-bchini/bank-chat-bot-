# Wifak Bank - Recruitment and Hiring Policy

**Document Reference:** HR-POL-009  
**Effective Date:** April 15, 2026  
**Version:** 2.5  
**Owner:** Talent Acquisition Division  

---

## 1. Objective and Equal Opportunity
Wifak Bank is committed to attracting, selecting, and retaining top talent to drive our business forward. We are dedicated to providing equal employment opportunities to all candidates. We hire based on merit, qualification, and competence, without regard to race, religion, gender, age, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality.

---

## 2. Hiring Process Workflow

### 2.1 Requisition & Authorization
* **Budget Check:** Before initiating a job search, hiring managers must obtain budget approval from their Department Head.
* **HR Approval:** The role must be logged in *TalentFlow* (Applicant Tracking System) with a defined Job Description, Salary Range, and Grade level (Grades 1 to 10).

### 2.2 Sourcing and Posting
* **Internal First:** All vacancies must be posted internally for a minimum of **5 business days** on the company intranet (*WifakJobs*) before external sourcing begins.
* **External Channels:** Roles may be posted on approved platforms (LinkedIn, Glassdoor, industry job boards) or outsourced to approved recruitment agencies.

### 2.3 Interview Stages
To ensure consistency and fairness, candidates must complete the following mandatory stages:
1. **Initial Screening (HR):** A 15-30 minute phone or video call conducted by a Talent Acquisition Specialist to assess basic qualifications and salary expectations.
2. **Technical / Role Assessment:** A standard test, case study, or live coding exercise (for tech roles) to verify functional competencies.
3. **Panel Interview:** A 60-minute interview with 2-3 key stakeholders, including the hiring manager and a peer from an adjacent department.
4. **Leadership / Culture Fit:** A final interview with the Department Head or VP to assess alignment with Wifak Bank values.

---

## 3. Employee Referral Program
Wifak Bank encourages employees to refer qualified external candidates for open positions.
* **Referral Bonus Structure:**
  * **Standard Roles (Grades 1-4):** **$1,500** bonus.
  * **Senior / Specialized Technical Roles (Grades 5-8):** **$3,000** bonus.
  * **Executive Roles (Grades 9+):** Non-eligible for referral bonuses.
* **Eligibility & Payout:**
  * The referral must be submitted via *TalentFlow* before the candidate's first interview.
  * The candidate must be hired and successfully complete their **90-day probationary period** before the bonus is paid.
  * Both the referring employee and the referee must be active employees at the time of payment.

---

## 4. Background Checks & Compliance
Given our status as a regulated financial institution, all job offers are strictly contingent upon successful completion of comprehensive background checks, which are conducted by an independent third-party provider (*VeriHire*):
* **Credit Check:** Mandatory for all roles with access to financial databases, treasury control, client accounts, or cash handling.
* **Criminal Record Check:** Required for all permanent, temporary, and contract hires.
* **Reference Check:** Verification of employment history and job titles for the past 5 years.
* **Education Verification:** Verification of university degrees and professional certifications claimed in the resume.
* **Offer Revocation:** Any discrepancies, false statements, or failed checks will result in the immediate revocation of the conditional job offer.
