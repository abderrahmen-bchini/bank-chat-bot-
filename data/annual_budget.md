**Document Reference:** FIN-BUD-2026  
**Effective Period:** January 1, 2026 - December 31, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0 (Approved)  
**Approved By:** Board of Directors & Chief Financial Officer (CFO)  

---

## 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the capital and operational budget allocation for Wifak Bank during the Fiscal Year 2026 (FY2026). The total budget is designed to support the bank's digital transformation roadmap, maintain strict regulatory compliance, scale retail banking operations, and implement robust cybersecurity controls.

Total projected operational expenditure (OpEx) is capped at **$24,800,000**, and capital expenditure (CapEx) is allocated at **$6,200,000**.

## 3. Capital Expenditure (CapEx) Breakdown
Total CapEx allocation is **$6,200,000**:
* **Infrastructure Upgrades ($3,000,000):** Migration of legacy database servers to AWS multi-region Aurora PostgreSQL clusters.
* **Security Hardware ($1,800,000):** Procurement of enterprise-grade firewalls, hardware security modules (HSMs) for encryption key storage, and secure developer laptops.
* **Office Facilities ($1,400,000):** Ergonomic office upgrades for the Chicago and London branches to support the hybrid work policy.

---

## 4. Financial Controls and Variances
To maintain financial discipline, the following controls are in place:
1. **Variance Tolerance:** Department heads are responsible for managing their spend. Any month-on-month budget variance exceeding **5%** must be reported to the Finance Department with a detailed explanation and remediation plan.
2. **Re-allocation Limits:** Inter-departmental budget transfers of up to **$50,000** require approval from the Finance Director. Transfers exceeding **$50,000** require approval from the CFO.
3. **Quarterly Review:** The budget will undergo formal quarterly reviews in April, July, and October to adjust allocations based on actual bank revenue and market conditions.
4. **Approval Thresholds:** Purchases or vendor agreements exceeding **$100,000** require dual signatures from the Department Head and the CFO.
